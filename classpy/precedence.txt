operator precedence

**		the exponent operator is given priority over all the others used in the expression

~ + -		the negation,unary plus and minus

* / // %	multiplication,divide, remainder, modulus,floor division

+ -		binary plus and mminus

>> <<		left shift, right shift

&		binary and

^ | 		binary xor,or

<= <> >=	comparision operator(less than,less than equal to,greater than,greater than equal to)

<> == !=	equality operators

= %= /=
//= -= += 
*= **=		assignment operator

is, is not	identity operator

in, not in	membership operator

not or and	logical operators
